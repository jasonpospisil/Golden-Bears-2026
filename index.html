⭐ Grand Fan Day

A day set aside to recognize grandparents or influential people in our players' lives. There will be special recognition and VIP passes for our GrandFans. Boys will autograph the special VIP Passes at a later practice. All Grand Fans pick up their lanyard at the tailgate tent and will receive a special sweet treat at the end of the game.

📋 Organization Expectations

For Our Athletes

★ Athletes should only wear team colors (Blue, Gold (yellow), White, Black, and Grey) on the field in the way of clothing and accessories. There is no restriction on cleat color.

For Our Families

★ Families volunteer during home games.
★ Be model spectators on the sidelines — no foul language, no yelling at the refs, no coaching your athletes from the sidelines, and no negative interactions with visiting spectators.
★ Tailgating responsibility — look after our shared space.
★ Leaving every space better than we found it.
★ No pets at games. Medical service animals are not included.
★ Being humble winners and losers.

🐻 The Golden Bear Way

We are a community first. Cheer loudly for our boys, support every family, and show up with good energy — on the field and off it. GO BEARS!
